The DataMite 4-Point Alignment Level

The 4-Point is a magnetic level that has two 360o vials with their axis 90o to one another. Being sealed in a polycarbonate case that is 1.4”x1.9”x.47” in size, produces a rugged, adaptive, simple, and fast level.
