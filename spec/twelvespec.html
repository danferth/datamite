DataMite 1250 Series General Specifications

SENSOR PROBES 1) Built_in piezoelectric construction, non-sparking stainless steel construction, coil corded w/BNC connector, extendable from 6" to 12 feet using P/N 8029 & 8023(6") and P/N 8029 & 8049 w/8124(12').
2) Type K surface thermocouple integrally wired to 1250 by coil cord, extendable from 6" to 12 feet using P/N 8023(6") and 8049w/8125.(12')
DISPLAY 3½ digits, ½" LCD, auto ranged for direct reading, ±1LSB and refresh rates of three per second. "BAT" symbol displays for recharging when low and then blanks to preserve data accuracy.
NUMERIC RANGES
Acoustic Emission : 0.0 to 70.0 decibels (dB)
Surface Temperature : 0 - 1500 oF (0 - 816oC)
Censorsound™ : 0 - 1999 Audible Sound
Ultrasound : 0 - 19.99 Supersonic sound Heterodyned
FREQUENCY RANGE Acoustic Emission; 20KHz TO 100 KHz, -3 dB points
Censorsound 40 Hz to 14.0 KHz, -3 Db points,
Ultrasonic 22 KHz to 57 KHz TUNABLE, -3 dB points translated to audible range.
TEMPERATURE RANGE 0o-1500oF (0o- 816oC) [ resolution of 1o]
General Accuracy: ±3% ±2 counts OF READING, operating temperature range of 0o to 50oC, Recalibration interval 1YR.
Temperature Accuracy: ±1oF (±1oC)over range -32oF - 1500oF (0oC to 816oC).
AVERAGING Depress and hold AVG/PWR button for five seconds to obtain an RMS average of the fluctuating input. On release the instrument reverts back to real time and resets the five minute battery saver circuits.
BATTERY SAVER Circuit turns instrument off after five minutes of non use. Restart by depressing AVG/PWR button momentarily.
BATTERY CONDITION Battery condition monitored continuously and displayed "BAT" symbol when charging is recommended. On continued use the circuit will BLANK the display preventing degraded data from being taken.
SIGNAL OUTPUT 0 to 2.0 VAC full scale for function selected. Source impedance: 1.0K ohms.
HEADPHONE OUTPUT 0 to 1.1 Volt RMS into <100 Ohm, current limited to .035 A. load, 3mm stereo jack connector.
POWER SOURCE Rechargeable: AA nickel cadmium rechargeable batteries. Operating time 48 hrs. continuous depending on headphone volume setting. A battery saver circuit turns instrument off after five minutes of non_use.
OPERATING
CONDITIONS 		Temperature: 32 oF to 122 oF (0o-50oC)
CONDITIONS Humidity: 95% RH non_condensing.
Storage:14 oF to 140oF. (-10oC to +60oC).
PHYSICAL 5¼"L x 5 1/16"W x 2¼"D (13.3cm x 12.8cm x 5.7cm) exclusive of probes.
Weight is 2.0 lb (930 g).
